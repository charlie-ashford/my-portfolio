At nineteen years old, Charlie Ashford has already established a remarkable presence in the developer community. Based in Sydney, Australia, Charlie specialises in creating tools that bring clarity to data — from YouTube analytics platforms to cross-platform social media trackers.

Currently serving as Extension Developer and NewStats lead at NewStudio, Charlie is shaping the future of YouTube analytics. Each project reflects a commitment to elegant interfaces, robust engineering, and the belief that data should be both accessible and beautiful.
