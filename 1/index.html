Profile — Who Is Charlie Ashford?

A 19-Year-Old Developer Building the Internet's Data Infrastructure

From real-time YouTube subscriber graphs to cross-platform analytics dashboards, Charlie Ashford has spent the last two years creating tools that thousands of people use to track, visualise, and understand online data. Now leading NewStats at NewStudio, he continues to push the boundaries of what web analytics can achieve. His portfolio spans 18 projects — bots, graphs, exporters, leaderboards, and more — each born from a desire to make data accessible, beautiful, and real-time.
